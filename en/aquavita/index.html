"Aqua vita is an innovative functional drink that is positioned in the growing market of healthy and low-calorie products. Our unique product combines water, fruits rich in vitamin C such as strawberries and raspberries, refreshing lemon juice, hibiscus flower with diuretic properties, and stevia as a natural zero-calorie sweetener. This carefully designed formula offers multiple benefits, including antioxidant properties, effective hydration, and a delicious natural flavor, all without adding excessive sugars or calories."

Aqua Vita was born to celebrate freshness. It represents a contemporary expression of healthy hydration, focusing on authentic and natural flavors. Made with high-quality ingredients and low sugar content, our drinks combine tradition and innovation to offer you a refreshing and conscious experience.

Entering at the end of 2024, Aqua vita launches its first products on the market, having a positive impact on its customers.
